WHAT IS
HOUSE OF PETALS?

Inspired by the founder of House of Petals, Joey House, The Real Life House of Petals located in the heart of Hollywood was a flower shop by day and a celebrity community Speakeasy by night, where many of Hollywood’s private jam sessions would occur. Frequent contributors at the House of Petals included the late Great Eddie Van Halen. His presence graced the stage with never before performances along with many iconic singers and musicians. He dedicated two very special guitars to House of Petals, in expression of the love, magic and art of this creative space. These guitars are priceless and very memorable. Joey House is gifting one of the guitars to a very special holder of the NFT collection as an expression of love and to keep Eddie’s legacy expanding with his core fans and more.

This one-of-kind Hollywood experience will be recreated in the House of Petals Metaverse. House of Petals will launch an exclusive NFT collection that will unlock many unique benefits and utilities to the community. The initial launch of 5150 House of Petals NFT “All Access Badges” will give its holders access to private celebrity events in Real Life (RL) and in the House of Petals Metaverse virtual experience. This collection is more than just a virtual collection since it is backed by physical assets like custom guitars, including the exclusive gifted and signed guitar by Eddie Van Halen.

House of Petals was and will be an environment of love and collaboration, invoking that raw creative energy that really fuels the art and music world. 10% of the proceeds from the sale of these NFT’s will be donated in perpetuity to City of Hope for cancer research and providing music lessons to underprivileged music students to continue the philanthropic legacy of the founding contributors.

WHAT IS AN NFT?

An NFT is a blockchain-based token that proves ownership of a digital item such as images, video files, and even physical assets.

WHAT IS MINTING AN NFT?

Minting an NFT refers to converting digital files into crypto collections or digital assets stored on the blockchain.

WHAT IS WHITELISTING?

Whitelisting is the process of getting a crypto wallet address pre-approved for a future NFT mint. The address being whitelisted would be your public Ethereum (ETH) address.

WHAT IS A HOP NFT AVATAR?

An avatar can be purchased after you mint two rare NFTs from our collection.

WHAT IS A HOP NFT BADGE?

A House of Petals NFT Badge is a minted NFT that gives Tier 1 access to the HOP community.
